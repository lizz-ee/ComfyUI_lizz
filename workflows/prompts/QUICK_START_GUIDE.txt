## IP-ADAPTER SETTINGS

- **Weight**: 0.4-0.5
  - Too low (< 0.3): Scene won't stay consistent
  - Too high (> 0.6): Character may be constrained/distorted
  - Sweet spot: 0.45

- **Model**: Use IP-Adapter FLUX model
  - Download from HuggingFace if you don't have it
  - Place in ComfyUI/models/ipadapter/

================================================================================

## TROUBLESHOOTING

**Problem**: Character is distorted or stuck in one pose
**Solution**: Lower IP-Adapter weight to 0.35-0.4

**Problem**: Scenery changes too much between frames
**Solution**: Increase IP-Adapter weight to 0.5-0.55

**Problem**: Background dominates, character details lost
**Solution**:
- Check LoRA is loaded at 0.85
- Lower IP-Adapter to 0.4
- Make character description more detailed in prompt

**Problem**: Scene Master backgrounds don't match style
**Solution**:
- Regenerate Scene Masters
- Make sure "Bob Ross painting style meets Arcane animation" is in prompt
- Try adding light LoRA (0.3) for Scene Masters
