## TECHNICAL SPECS

**Total Keyframes**: 15 frames
**Recommended Interpolation**:
- 16 frames between each keyframe = 240 total frames
- At 24fps = 10 seconds of footage
- At 30fps = 8 seconds of footage

**Camera Movement Notes**:
- Frames 1-3: Slow pull-back (crane up and away)
- Frames 4-6: Tilt down, following subject
- Frames 7-10: Wide static shots (let her fall away from camera)
- Frames 11-15: Dynamic following shots (camera falls with her)

**Color Grading Journey**:
- Start: Warm golden hour (hope, contemplation)
- Middle: Balanced warm/cool (transition, release)
- End: Cool atmospheric blues (infinity, peace)

**Music Suggestions**:
- Slow, contemplative piano opening
- Swelling strings as camera pulls back
- Weightless, ambient soundscape during fall
- No impact/ending - loop or fade to white/clouds

## ANIMATION INTERPOLATION STRATEGY

### Method 1: RIFE (Recommended)
1. Import 15 keyframes in sequence
2. Set interpolation: 16x between frames
3. Export at 24fps
4. Result: Smooth 10-second sequence

### Method 2: After Effects
1. Import keyframes as sequence
2. Apply "Pixel Motion Blur"
3. Time remapping for slow-motion feel
4. Add subtle camera shake/motion blur

### Method 3: AI Video Tools
- **Runway Gen-3**: Upload keyframes, specify "falling through air, smooth motion"
- **Pika Labs**: Great for character animation interpolation
- **Kling AI**: Excellent at maintaining character consistency
