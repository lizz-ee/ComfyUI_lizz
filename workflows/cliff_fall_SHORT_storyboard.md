1. **Generate 13 keyframes** using FLUX workflow (Frames 01-13)

   - Target: ~15-20 seconds forward sequence
3. **Speed up and reverse Part 1** in editing software
   - Reverse footage
   - Speed up to ~3 seconds (VCR rewind effect)
